Yining Yuan

About

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Bristol. My main research interest is norm evolution in multiagent systems, while I am also interested in multiagent reinforcement learning, explainability, and ethical AI. I graduated with a BSc in Information Management and Systems in 2023 from Dalian University of Technology, and a MSc Data Science in 2024 from University of Bristol. During this journey, my interest in designing agents that collaborate effectively in socio-technical systems continues to grow. Now, I am associated with Collective Dynamics Lab under the supervision of Dr. Nirav Ajmeri and Prof. Weiru Liu.

Currently, I am also working at Ask-JGI helpdesk to support statistical modelling and data analysis for researchers across University of Bristol.

See my CV or contact me.
