About

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Bristol. I graduated with a BSc in Information Management and Systems in 2023 from Dalian University of Technology, and an MSc in Data Science in 2024 from the University of Bristol.

My work focuses on norm evolution in multi-agent systems, and I am also interested in multi-agent reinforcement learning, explainability, and ethical AI. I am associated with Collective Dynamics Lab under the supervision of Dr. Nirav Ajmeri and Prof. Weiru Liu.

I also work at the Ask-JGI helpdesk, supporting statistical modelling and data analysis for researchers across the University of Bristol.

See my CV for more details on my research.
